President’s Message

Hello and welcome.

On behalf of the Global Brotherhood Basketball Association (GBBA), I would like to extend my sincere appreciation to all of you visiting our website.

The GBBA was founded with a clear mission — to promote inclusivity, sportsmanship, and the empowerment of individuals through the game of basketball across all generations and cultures.

In today’s world, the role of unity and community in sports is more vital than ever. Basketball offers a powerful platform for connection, teamwork, and growth — values that transcend gender, age, or background. Through basketball, we learn resilience, respect, and joy.

At GBBA, we believe in equal opportunity for all — whether you are a young aspiring player, a veteran coach, or simply a passionate supporter. Our programs aim to foster development through international tournaments, grassroots initiatives, and leadership training for coaches and referees.

We host annual global and regional competitions, and our certified education programs ensure that our participants are both skilled and ethically grounded. We are proud to see our members grow, lead, and inspire others both on and off the court.

We invite you to join us in building a more connected and healthier world through basketball. Your support helps us shape future generations who will carry forward the spirit of the game — with integrity, passion, and purpose.

Thank you for being part of our journey.

Alex Raymond
President, GBBA
