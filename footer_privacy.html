Privacy Policy

  • This is the Privacy Policy for the Global Basketball Association (GBBA) website. All personal information handled by GBBA is collected, retained, and processed based on relevant laws. GBBA abides by the Personal Information Protection Act and processes personal data lawfully and appropriately to protect users' rights and ensure the proper execution of tasks. Users can exercise their rights regarding their personal data, and in case of infringement, may request administrative appeals as stipulated by law. In accordance with Article 30 of the Personal Information Protection Act, GBBA establishes and discloses this Privacy Policy to ensure user rights and provide a streamlined way to handle user concerns.

  • Article 1 (Purpose of Personal Information Processing)
    1. GBBA processes personal information for the following purposes only.
    2. Data will not be used for other purposes without separate consent as per Article 18 of the Act.
    • a. Membership Registration and Management: Identification, authentication, legal guardian consent verification (under age 14), satisfaction surveys, etc.
    • b. Inquiry Handling: Viewing, correcting/deleting, processing suspension requests, and incident reports regarding personal data.
  • Article 2 (Retention Period)
    1. GBBA retains personal data only for the period defined by law and for achieving its stated purposes.
    No. File Name Data Items Retention Period
    1 Regular and Associate Members Name, Nickname, Phone, Email During membership
  • Article 3 (Provision to Third Parties)
    1. Personal information is only provided to third parties with user consent or under legal obligations as per Articles 17 and 18 of the Act.
  • Article 4 (User Rights and Methods of Exercising Them)
    1. Users (or their legal guardians) may exercise the following rights at any time:
    2. Submit a written, email, or fax request using the official form (Annex Form 8). GBBA will respond without delay.
    3. If correction/deletion is requested, data will not be used until completed.
    4. Requests may be made by a representative (Annex Form 11).
    5. Rights may be restricted under Articles 35(5) and 37(2).
    6. If collection is legally mandated, deletion may not be allowed.
    7. GBBA will verify if the requester is the subject or an authorized representative.
  • Article 5 (Processed Data Items)
    1. GBBA collects and uses the following items:
      • a. Membership Registration
        1. 1) Regular members: Name, Birthdate, Gender, Phone, Email, Mailing, SMS
        2. 2) Coaches: Name, Affiliation, Birthdate, Gender, Phone, Email, Mailing, SMS (After ID verification)
    2. Outsourced contracts explicitly restrict sharing and ensure protection. Changes will be notified publicly.
  • Article 6 (Data Disposal Procedure and Method)
    1. Data is disposed of without delay when no longer needed or upon member withdrawal.
    2. Even after the purpose is met, legal obligations may require separate, secure storage.
    3. Disposal is as follows:
      • a. Procedure

        Disposal is based on internal disposal plans and requires approval from the Data Protection Officer.

      • b. Method

        Electronic files are deleted irreversibly. Paper files are shredded or incinerated.

  • Article 7 (Security Measures)
    1. GBBA implements the following safeguards:
      • a. Administrative: Internal policies, regular staff training
      • b. Technical: Access control, encryption, security software
      • c. Physical: Server room and archive access control
  • Article 8 (Cookies)
    1. Cookies are used to provide personalized services.
    2. Cookies store small information pieces in the user's browser.
      • a. Purpose

        Track site usage, popular searches, and secure login status.

      • b. Rejection

        (1) Configure via Tools > Internet Options > Privacy.

        (2) Rejecting cookies may limit service usage.
