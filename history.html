History

1891

Basketball invented by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, USA
1932

Fédération Internationale de Basketball (FIBA) founded in Geneva, Switzerland
1946

Basketball Association of America (BAA), precursor to NBA, is established
1992

USA Dream Team participates in the Barcelona Olympics, globalizing the sport
2010

Growth of international leagues and players in NBA reshapes the global landscape
2020

GBBA (Global Basketball Association) is established to promote basketball worldwide
2022

Inaugural GBBA World Championship held in Manila, Philippines
2024

GBBA Youth Development Program launched across 5 continents
